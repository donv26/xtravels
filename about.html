    XA Travels Owner

    Happiness G. Moshi was born and raised at the Safari hub Arusha, Tanzania. She went to Dar-es-Salaam University for her Undergraduate degree in Commerce and Management and then Mzumbe University for Masters of Science in Human Resource Management. She has been a Human Resource Manager for five years with Constructing Comapny, Life Insurance Company and Manufacturing Companies. Got married and they have a lovely Daughter . Her husband who is the Expedition Leader for many years took her to her first finest guided tour in Serengeti and she saw Elephants, Lions, Cheetah, Leopard, Buffalo and a lot of Birds and many more animals, then the Balloon Safari over the Great Migration was a breath taking experience that she will never forget. She spend nights in a Luxury Tented camp close to the nature as her first experience we had Hippos and Buffalos around the camp just being separated by a canvas from all the animals. That life changing experience she had she want to share with the rest of the world by arranging and operating tours for their guests in Tanzania, Kenya, Rwanda, Uganda, Botswana and South Africa

    • About XA Travels Company

      Xatsa African Travels is a registered travel company based in Arusha,Tanzania. Our experienced guides are travel enthusiasts who have worked in the tourism industry for over 20 years. Our clientele includes: Ivy Leagues, Dartmouth Alumni, Toyota World Discovery, Immension Journey, Texas A&M, Overseas Adventure Travel, Cincinnati Zoo and Botanical Garden, Tauck World Discovery and Bridges, Orbridge, Young Presidential Organization, World Presidential Organization, Wisconsin Alumni, Napel Zoo, Ohio State University, Michigan State University, Acadia Birding Festival, and University of North Carolina.

      Xatsa is an Iraqw word meaning “wilderness.” Xatsa African Travel (or Xa Travels) is a luxury experiential travel company that was born from our deep love of wilderness, and our responsibility for its protection. We create personalized itineraries throughout Africa based on Africa conservation and positive impact to local communities.

      We specialize in taking guests on safari in East to Southern Africa offering: great migration safaris, unlimited game drives, guided walks, beach holidays, gorilla trekking, chimpanzee trekking, Kilimanjaro hiking, & night game drives.

      Our adventures offer options for families and solo travelers, as well as honeymooner specials. Our guests enjoy exceptional encounters with abundant wildlife, large mammals, and smaller creatures, as well as exploring important plants, habitats and ecosystems of the land. Our guides will interpret the remarkable secrets hidden within this natural environment and connect you to nature.
