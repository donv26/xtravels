    Best of Botswana




    Botswana's Chobe & Delta Duration: 7 Days

    Days 1-2

    Victoria Falls from Zambia

    Arrival to Livingstone Airport, Zambia Road transfer to Sussi & Chuma Lodge Two nights stay at Lodge If you aren’t taken aback by the gorgeous river view from your tree house, you have a game viewing adventure to spot rhinos coming your way. Game drives and walking safaris are popular in Mosi-oa Tunya National Park, conducted by the lodge.
    Touring the Victorian Falls within the Zambian territory, you can enjoy cruise rides, or you can try canoeing. In this adventure capital of Africa, you can swim in the devil’s pool, which is a natural hollow hanging over the cliff, enjoy bungee jumping or take a helicopter ride. There is much to choose from!

    Days 3-4

    Chobe National Park Safari

    Check out from Sussi & Chuma Lodge post breakfast Over the river Border crossing to Kazangula Drive to Chobe Chilwero Lodge Two nights stay at lodge
    You will be staying above the Chobe River overlooking the border between four countries. In this second largest reserve for wild in the country, its massive elephant population is most renowned.
    Along with big game safaris consisting of hippos, lions, and crocodiles you also get to witness herds of buffalos. Activities include; hi-teas at safaris, river cruise, and the lodge offers one of the best spa treatments.

    Days 5-6

    Okavango Delta

    Transfer to Kasane airport Flight to Stanley’s Airstrip Road Transfer to Stanley’s Camp Two nights stay at camp Luxury tents, high-class safari and exploring the wild, this is what the last few days are about. No more than 16 guests are entertained at a single time so you can expect privacy and personalized service.
    You are guided through nature walks, enjoy night drives which are rarely offered, go on game drives, have romantic dinners under the stars and canoe excursions depending on the water levels. You also have the chance to feed elephants that stay under the camp’s care.

    Day 7

    Bye Bye Botswana
    Safari Morning Breakfast Drive to Stanley’s Airstrip Flight to Maun International Airport Back to your journey One last game drive safari is a must. Starting early in the morning right before you leave so you can keep your memories fresh before you carry your adventure without us and leave back home.
